License Services in Dubai

Complete Business License Solutions for UAE Businesses

The process of getting a business license in the UAE involves getting approvals from the government and DED (Department of Economic Development). Understanding and navigating these complex regulatory requirements can be challenging, especially for new business owners unfamiliar with UAE procedures.

At LedgerWorx, we specialize in all types of license services and guide you through the entire process from application to approval and renewal with confidence and expertise.

Types of Licenses We Provide

  • Trade licenses for commercial businesses
  • Industrial licenses
  • Professional licenses
  • Tourism licenses
  • Labor licenses and approvals
  • License renewal and amendments
  • Activity change applications
  • Free zone licenses
  • Offshore licenses

Our License Service Process

We handle every aspect of your business license from initial consultation to final approval. Our team works closely with DED and other relevant authorities to ensure smooth processing and timely delivery.
