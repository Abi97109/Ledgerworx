Corporate Sponsorship Services

Expert Sponsorship Solutions for Your Business

Corporate sponsorship forms a crucial backbone of UAE business operations. The corporate sponsorship gives better insurance to your general business. Even in the unfortunate event that the UAE national's employment contract expires, your business operations remain unaffected.

Any new foreign company can become a corporate sponsor of a UAE company that employs at least one UAE national. At LedgerWorx, we provide expert guidance and assistance in navigating the corporate sponsorship process with full compliance and efficiency.

Why You Need Corporate Sponsorship

  • Provides business continuity and stability
  • Enables foreign companies to sponsor visas
  • Ensures full legal compliance with UAE regulations
  • Protects business interests in case of visa changes
  • Allows employment of UAE nationals
  • Facilitates smooth business operations
  • Enables business growth and expansion
  • Improves business credibility and standing

Our Corporate Sponsorship Services

We handle all aspects of corporate sponsorship including initial setup, visa sponsorship approvals, renewal, and modifications. Our experienced team works with all relevant authorities to ensure smooth processing and compliance.

Whether you're setting up as a new corporate sponsor or managing existing sponsorships, LedgerWorx provides comprehensive support, expert guidance, and handles all documentation and regulatory requirements.
